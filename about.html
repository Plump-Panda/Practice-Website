About Us

Here at The Reviews we all love anime. We are a dedicated group of bloggers that love to watch and review anime. Our group consists of seven people and we all write reviews about the things we think are worth criticizing or praising!

What We Do

As soon as a new anime movie or tv show finishes you can count on us to give you our honest feedback. We review practically anything we can. We review what some may consider "normie shows" all the way to the most obscure shows you can think of. There will be new posts everyweek so make sure to stick around!

How We Started

We all met online through other blogging websites. As time went on all of us started to become friends and it turned out that we all shared an interst in talking about and reviewing anime. After a while, we decided that we would create our own blogging website that would host our own personal reviews, and here we are!
